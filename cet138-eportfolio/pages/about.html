About Me

Academic Learner and Working Professional

My name is Pradip Gaire. I am currently studying in the Second Semester at ISMT College, Chitwan with a focus on Computer Systems. I believe in balancing theory and practice, and I try to apply my classroom learning in real work situations.

My academic journey in Computer Systems introduced me to web technologies, problem-solving methods, and the importance of logical thinking. Through the CET138 module, I learned how frontend structure, visual design, framework support, and scripting work together to build complete web applications.

I am developing a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. I enjoy practical tasks because they help me understand concepts deeply and improve confidence for future development projects.

Professional Experience
